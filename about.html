About

Hi, I'm Tyler.
Let's make something real.

LA born and raised (a rarity, I know). Wife to my best friend, mama to Parker and Camden, and dog mom to Dunken, the world's smushiest French Bulldog. I love donuts and pineapples, the beach, baking, and being completely goofy. My friends would describe me as warm, laid-back, and fiercely loyal. That's exactly how I show up for my couples.

I started Tyler Chase Collective because I was tired of wedding photos that looked like everyone else's: stiff, predictable, posed. I wanted to make images that felt like the day actually felt. So I show up like a close friend who happens to have a camera. I blend in, I watch, and then I hand you a gallery that makes you say "I didn't even notice you capturing that."

I respond fast, usually within the hour, unless I'm asleep. If something goes sideways on your day, I'll handle it. I take on fewer clients so I can actually show up for each one.

How I Work

You don't need to know
what to do.

Here's something most photographers won't tell you: the photos that look the most candid are almost always directed. I give every couple clear, gentle prompts throughout the day: where to walk, how to hold each other, what to do with their hands, so you're never just standing there wondering what to do.

They turn out natural because I worked to get you there. You stay relaxed, you stay in the moment, and the photos show it.

Most of my couples tell me they forgot I was there. That's what I'm going for.
