Common Questions

Everything you're probably wondering.

Do you travel for weddings?

Yes. We're based in Southern California with a second home base in Hawaii, and we shoot weddings across the country and internationally. Travel fees apply for locations beyond 100 miles; we'll be upfront about costs before anything is finalized.

How do we officially book our date?

Once we confirm your date is available, you'll sign a simple agreement and pay a 50% retainer to lock it in. The remainder is due 2 weeks before your wedding.

What if we need to postpone?

Date changes are allowed within one month of signing your contract, subject to availability. Things come up. We'll do our best to work with you within that window.

Do you shoot RAW files? Can we have them?

Yes, we shoot exclusively in RAW. We deliver fully edited JPEGs, each one hand-edited and color graded to match the actual light of your day. The RAW files aren't included, but your edited gallery is yours to keep.

Do you work with associate photographers?

Yes. On select dates, or when I'm already booked, I work with a small team of associate photographers who I've personally trained in my style and approach. Every associate gallery is reviewed and edited by me before delivery. Same quality, same 4-week turnaround..

What's the 4-week delivery mean exactly?

Within 4 weeks of your wedding date, your full hand-edited gallery will be in your inbox. Not an estimate. That's the actual deadline we hold ourselves to.
